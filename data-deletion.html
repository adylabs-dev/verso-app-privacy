Privacy · Your Rights

Request Data Deletion

We respond within 30 days · GDPR Article 17 — Right to Erasure

No account needed. Verso does not require sign-up. Most data is stored locally on your device and is deleted automatically when you uninstall the app.

How to request deletion

Send us an email with the subject line below. We will delete all server-side data associated with your anonymous user ID within 30 days and confirm by reply.

Tap to open your email app

adylabs.android@gmail.com

Subject: Data Deletion Request — Verso

What gets deleted

DataWhere storedAction
Anonymous user ID (Firebase) Google Firebase servers Deleted on request
Crash reports & analytics Firebase Crashlytics / Analytics Auto-deleted after 14 months
Ad interaction data Google AdMob Managed by Google
Your PDF files Your device only Uninstall the app
App preferences & recent files list Your device only Uninstall the app
Subscription status Google Play Managed by Google Play

Uninstalling the app

Uninstalling Verso from your device removes all locally stored data immediately — your PDF files, preferences, recent file history, and cached thumbnails. No email needed for local data.

For data held by Google (AdMob, Firebase Analytics, Google Play), you can also manage your preferences directly through Google's privacy controls.
