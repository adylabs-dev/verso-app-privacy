What we do NOT collect

  • We do not collect your name, email address, or any account credentials (no login required).
  • We do not read, upload, or store your PDF files on our own servers. File processing is performed locally on your device unless you explicitly invoke an AI feature.
  • We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data with any third party for their own marketing purposes.
  • We do not use cross-app tracking or build advertising profiles beyond what Google AdMob requires to serve contextual ads.
3

AI Features & Data Processing

When you use Flashcards, Translate, Listen (Audio Summary), or Multi-Doc Chat, Verso extracts text from the PDF you have opened and sends it to a third-party AI service provider to generate responses.

  • What is sent: Only the text content of the pages you process — no file metadata, no account identifiers.
  • What is not sent: Images, annotations, signatures, or any file that has not been explicitly submitted by you.
  • Retention by AI provider: Data is processed transiently. Third-party providers operate under their own privacy policies. We select providers that commit to not training models on user data submitted via their API.
  • Your control: AI features are fully optional. You can use all other Verso tools without any data leaving your device.
Before sending any PDF content to an AI service, Verso will display a prompt describing what will be processed. You can cancel at any time.

Data Retention

  • Crash & analytics data — retained by Google Firebase for up to 14 months, then automatically deleted.
  • Advertising data — managed by Google AdMob per Google's data retention policies.
  • Subscription tokens — retained only as long as you hold an active subscription. Tokens are invalidated by Google Play when a subscription expires or is cancelled.
  • AI processing content — not retained by us. Retention by the AI provider is governed by their API data handling terms (typically discarded after the response is generated).
  • Local app data (preferences, recent files list) — stored on your device. Deleted when you uninstall the app.

Permissions

Verso requests the following Android permissions:

PermissionWhy it's needed
READ_MEDIA_IMAGES / READ_EXTERNAL_STORAGE To open PDF files and images from your device storage
WRITE_EXTERNAL_STORAGE To save processed PDFs back to your device (Android 9 and below)
CAMERA To capture document pages using the Scanner tool — only activated when you tap "Scan"
INTERNET To display ads, send crash reports, verify subscriptions, and use AI features

You can revoke camera and storage permissions at any time in Android Settings → Apps → Verso → Permissions. Revoking storage access will prevent the app from opening files.

Your Rights Under GDPR

As a resident of the European Economic Area, you have the following rights regarding your personal data:

Right of Access (Art. 15) Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
Right to Rectification (Art. 16) Ask us to correct inaccurate or incomplete data.
Right to Erasure (Art. 17) Request deletion of your personal data ("right to be forgotten").
Right to Restriction (Art. 18) Ask us to pause processing of your data in certain circumstances.
Right to Portability (Art. 20) Receive your data in a machine-readable format.
Right to Object (Art. 21) Object to processing based on legitimate interest, including profiling.
Withdraw Consent (Art. 7) Withdraw any consent you have given at any time without penalty.
Lodge a Complaint (Art. 77) File a complaint with your national supervisory authority.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at amagahabdeladim@gmail.com. We will respond within 30 days. For data held by Google (Firebase, AdMob, Play Billing), requests must be directed to Google LLC via their privacy tools.

Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organisational measures to protect your data against unauthorised access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. These include:

  • All data in transit is encrypted using TLS 1.2 or higher.
  • Firebase services are secured by Google's enterprise-grade infrastructure.
  • We do not store payment card data — all billing is handled entirely by Google Play.
  • PDF files you process remain on your device unless you deliberately invoke an AI feature.

No method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. While we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your data, we cannot guarantee its absolute security.
